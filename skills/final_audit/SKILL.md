Final audit checklist:
- Output only the final manuscript Markdown.
- Remove planning notes, meta commentary, and internal reasoning.
- Confirm every patient-specific fact can be traced to the source case.
- Confirm every figure is rendered as Markdown image syntax, for example `![Figure n](images/<filename>)`.
- Confirm every image link uses the image index path.
- Confirm references are verified by the source case or clearly marked as unavailable.
- Keep language precise, concise, and publication-ready.
