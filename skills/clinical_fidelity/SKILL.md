---
name: clinical_fidelity
description: Keep all clinical writing grounded in the supplied case record.
when_to_use: Use for every clinical case report stage.
---
Rules:
- Treat the source case as the only authority for patient-specific facts.
- Preserve exact demographics, measurements, dates, timelines, doses, procedures, test results, diagnoses, and outcomes.
- Do not smooth over messy clinical reality such as delays, missed visits, complications, or uncertain findings.
- Separate confirmed facts from interpretation.
- If a detail is absent, write around it instead of inventing it.
- Do not provide treatment advice for a real patient.
