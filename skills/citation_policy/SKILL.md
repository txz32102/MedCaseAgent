---
name: citation_policy
description: Prevent invented literature and unsupported references.
when_to_use: Use whenever references, novelty, background, or literature comparison may appear.
---
Citation rules:
- Use only references, DOIs, PMIDs, URLs, or bibliography entries present in the supplied case record.
- Do not invent authors, article titles, journal names, years, DOIs, PMIDs, guideline names, or reference strings.
- If the case record contains no verified references, keep background claims general and uncited.
- If a References section is necessary but no verified references were supplied, state that no verified references were supplied rather than fabricating entries.
- Literature comparison must be conservative: prefer "rare", "unusual", or "limited reports" only when supported by the source.
